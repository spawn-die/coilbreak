How to play

  • WASD move
  • Mouse aim · Click fire
  • F / RMB plant coil nodes
  • Space phase dash
  • 123 pick upgrades

Fantasy

You are a Riftwalker trapped in a collapsing neon pocket. Link energy coils into cutting beams, harvest essence, evolve your loadout, and break the Core Warden before the void takes you.

Systems

Combat — bolts, dash i-frames, contact damage.
Coils — place nodes; beams shred anything between them (costs energy).
Progression — clear waves → choose upgrades → face the Warden on wave 5.
